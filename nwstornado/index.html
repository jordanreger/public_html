@nwstornado.bsky.social

@nwstornado.bsky.social is a bot I built to be a rough mirror of @NWSTornado on Twitter. I am not a fan of Twitter, as I'm sure most people aren't anymore, but it was an extremely useful tool in some cases. I am a big fan of protocol-level sovereignty and Bluesky is building out that dream as we speak. You can view the code for the bot at ~jordanreger/nwstornado.
