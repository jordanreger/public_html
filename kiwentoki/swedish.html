Structure: subject + verb (+ object)

Example: mi ken moku e kiwen pi kule ala. ni li pakala ala e mi. ↔ jag kan äta glas. det inte skadar mig.

Conjugation

Here are some examples of when it is necesary to conjugate. I'd recommend using only "I" and "they" for everything:

toki pona Swedish
mi ken jag kan
ona ken hen kan
